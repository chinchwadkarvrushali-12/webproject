Heat Cure

HeatCure is an advanced thermal glass coating solution designed to enhance indoor comfort while maximizing energy efficiency. With HeatCure, you no longer need to worry about excessive heat entering your premises or compromising on natural daylight.

This transparent nano-technology coating forms a durable, invisible layer on glass surfaces that effectively blocks heat-causing radiation while allowing optimal light transmission. It requires no regular maintenance and offers long-lasting performance.

HeatCure provides reliable protection from infrared (IR) and ultraviolet (UV) rays during summer and acts as an insulator in winter, maintaining comfort year-round.

With a shelf life of up to 12 years, HeatCure delivers consistent performance and long-term value.

Key Benefits of HeatCure Glass Coating

  • Blocks up to 85% Infrared (IR) Heat
  • Blocks up to 99% Ultraviolet (UV) Rays
  • Reduces indoor heat and glare
  • Saves energy & lowers cooling costs
  • Reduces condensation on glass surfaces
  • Works as a thermal insulator
  • Maintains natural light & clear visibility
  • 10+ years of warranty & durability
