About Us

Yasha hydraulics, established in 2001, deals in spares for earth-moving, mining, and construction equipment, including Hydraulic Rock Breaker spares, Transit Mixers, and Vibrating Compactors.
We are the Authorized Dealer for Chicago Pneumatic Construction Equipment, DTR Equipment, Yanmar Mini Excavator covering West Maharashtra and Konkan.
We offer Hydraulic Rock Breakers, Portable Compressors, Light Towers and more.
We stock a wide range of spares for excavators, representing some of the best manufacturers in the country.
Our inventory includes:
• Chicago Pneumatic construction equipments
• Dozco make ground engaging and under carriage parts
• Donaldson Filters – Oil / Air / Diesel Filters
• Transit Mixers & Concrete Pump Parts
• Kirloskar oil engines genuine spares.
• Parker and brilon Make hydraulics hose assemblies
• HS Brand under carriage parts.
• Yanmar Mini Excavator spare parts.
