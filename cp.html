Electric Portable Compressors

Explore our electric portable air compressor range designed to deliver robust performance and energy efficiency. With airflow capacities ranging from 300 to 600 CFM and pressure levels between 100 to 185 PSI, these compressors are powered by high-efficiency TEFC electric motors ranging from 45kW to 110kW. The motor and element combination is carefully optimized to ensure reliability, toughness, and superior portability, making them ideal for demanding applications. Built with a rugged undercarriage, these compressors are easy to manoeuvre across tough terrains, ensuring smooth operation even in challenging environments. Safety is a priority — the plug-and-play socket system comes with an emergency switch for enhanced protection during use. Additionally, their low carbon dioxide emissions contribute to a cleaner, more environmentally responsible footprint, aligning with modern sustainability goals.
