HEAT CURE

Protect your home interiors from harmful UV rays with smart glass solutions. Heat Cure is a transparent nano-technology coating that forms a durable, invisible layer on glass surfaces.

It blocks heat-causing infrared (IR) and ultraviolet (UV) radiation while allowing natural light to pass through, keeping interiors cooler and safer.

Services of Heat Cure

  • Roof Coating / RCC Coating
  • Industrial Shade Coating / Metal Coating

Applications

  • Pre-engineered metal buildings – factory, warehouse, supermarket, showrooms, aircraft hangar, etc.
  • Public and residential buildings – wall and roof insulation, facade, drywall, and acoustic panels.
  • Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning – duct-thermal, acoustic, and mechanical rooms.
  • Textile mills – roofing and humidification duct.
  • Transport – trains, metro coaches, railway coaches, AC buses.
  • Automotive insulation (moulded) parts.
  • Marine – naval shipbuilding.
  • Cold storage.
  • Solar – water heater and tank insulation.
  • Power plants – chimney, ESP, water pipes, and vessels.
  • Cryogenic applications – LNG tanks.
  • Other various appliances and laboratory equipment.
  • BS 6853 (toxicity index 0.65) – NON-EMISSION OF SMOKE and TOXIC GASES.

Key Benefits of HeatCure Glass Coating

  • High thermal and acoustic insulation values at a much lower weight.
  • Non-combustible and fire-safe insulation to meet fire and life safety requirements of the National Building Code 2016.
  • Easy to carry, lay and install – fast, hassle-free application.
  • Extra strong facings for better durability and ease of installation.
  • Long length for minimum joints and sealing – less man-hour.
  • Silver, white and black coloured finish for different site requirements.
  • Apply and forget – maintenance-free.
  • Safe from acidic vapours with strong polypropylene facing.
  • Free from metal shots and corrosive chlorides – better life of roof sheets.
  • Efficient compressed packaging for better storage space management and less freight cost.
  • Available in standard yellow material (Twiga Insul) and soft-feel green material (Twiga Eco Insul).
  • Can be used in recording studios, dubbing rooms, auditoriums, hotels, residential areas, commercial spaces, jam rooms, cinema halls, server rooms, ship & yacht building, Voice Over (VO) rooms, etc.
  • Isover Acoustic Glasswool Roll Specification.
