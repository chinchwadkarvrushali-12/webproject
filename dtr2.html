DTR - Applicable Carrier Models
DTR hydraulics breakers are designed for high-performance rock breaking, concrete demolition, and excavation work. They are built for durability and efficiency, making them ideal for excavators, backhoes, and skid steers in tough working conditions.

Benefits

  • High Productivity: Nitrogen Charged backhead for superior impact transfer.
  • Efficient Demolition: Large diameter piston for high impact energy.
  • R.O.I.: Low cost of operation & maintenance with original DTR parts.
  • Long Service Life: Durable with high cost benefit.
  • Safety: Robust casing protects inner body from damage.

Applicable Carrier Models

  • 4-8T Models
  • 10-15T Models
  • 17-26T Models
  • 28-38T Models
